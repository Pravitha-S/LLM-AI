Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our hospital services

🏥 General

Is treatment free at District General Hospital? +
Yes! Consultation is free for all patients. Medicines, diagnostics and surgery are free for beneficiaries of Karuna Health Scheme, Arogyakeralam, Ayushman Bharat PMJAY, JSSK and BPL card holders.
What are the hospital working hours? +
OPD is open Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Emergency services are available 24 hours, 7 days a week including Sundays and holidays.
Is the hospital open on Sundays? +
OPD is closed on Sundays. However, Emergency Department, ICU, Blood Bank and pharmacy are available round the clock including Sundays.

🎫 Appointments & Tokens

How do I get an OPD token? +
Visit the OPD counter between 8:00 AM and 11:00 AM to collect a token. You can also book online through our website. Token collection is completely free.
How do I check my token status? +
Enter your token number (format: KSD followed by 4 digits, e.g. KSD2045) in the Token Status checker on our homepage, or ask our AI chatbot. You can also check at the OPD reception display board.
Can I book an appointment in advance? +
Yes, advance appointment booking is available online for specialist consultations. Walk-in tokens are also available daily at the OPD counter from 8AM.

📋 Documents & Schemes

What documents do I need to bring? +
For general consultation: Any valid ID (Aadhaar, voter ID). For government scheme benefits: Aadhaar card + ration card. For Ayushman Bharat: PMJAY card or Aadhaar linked to scheme.
How do I know if I'm eligible for free treatment? +
BPL card holders, Karuna scheme beneficiaries, Arogyakeralam members, PMJAY card holders and JSSK beneficiaries (pregnant women and newborns) are eligible. Visit the scheme desk at the entrance for verification.

🚑 Emergency

How do I call an ambulance? +
Call 108 for free ambulance service (24/7) or 102 for maternal ambulance service. You can also reach our emergency directly at 04994-220332.
Is blood available at all times? +
Yes, our blood bank operates 24/7 with all blood groups available. Contact blood bank at 04994-220331. Voluntary donation is always welcome.
